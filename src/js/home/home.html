Quantifying feedback, one request at a time This forum provides an open communications channel between Product and Sales to help shape the direction of the product ecosystem and guide the priority of feature roadmaps through quantifiable requests and customer attribution.

How it Works

  • Add a feature request
  • Associate a customer and account value with the request
  • Product will review and weigh the business value
  • You will be contacted with followup questions in the comments
  • You can track the status of the request at any time or associate additional customers
  • Items with the greatest customer value move to the top of the list
